About the Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Established with a forward-thinking vision, the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at our institution stands as a beacon of technological excellence and innovation. Our department is committed to providing high-quality technical education, fostering innovation, and preparing students for challenging careers in technology.

Key Highlights

  • State-of-the-art infrastructure and advanced computational facilities
  • Experienced and highly qualified faculty with extensive industry and academic expertise
  • Industry-aligned curriculum that bridges academic learning with practical technological demands
  • Strong emphasis on research, innovation, and entrepreneurial skills
  • Comprehensive training programs to enhance student employability

Department Mission

• Impart knowledge by providing a healthy learning environment. • Encourage the zeal to be updated in technology front and abide by ethical and moral values. • Engage in result-oriented research and innovation to cater to societal needs.

Department Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in the field of Computer Science and Engineering, imparting holistic education catering to the needs of society.

Program Outcomes and Objectives ▼

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs) ▼

  1. To produce graduates who will excel in professional careers in Computer Science and Engineering or pursue higher education.
  2. To develop professionals with strong technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and ethical values.
  3. Cultivate lifelong learning and adaptability in rapidly evolving technological landscapes.
  4. Foster innovation, research, and entrepreneurial mindset among students.
  5. Prepare students to become global professionals who contribute meaningfully to societal challenges.

Program Outcomes (POs) ▼

  1. Engineering Knowledge: Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and computing fundamentals to solve complex engineering problems.
  2. Problem Analysis: Identify, formulate, research literature, and analyze complex computing problems reaching substantive conclusions.
  3. Design/Development of Solutions: Design solutions for complex computing problems and design systems, components, or processes to meet specified needs.
  4. Conduct Investigations: Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis, and interpretation of data.
  5. Modern Tool Usage: Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern computing tools.
  6. Professional Ethics: Apply reasoning informed by contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues.
  7. Sustainability and Environment: Understand the impact of professional computing solutions in environmental and societal contexts.
  8. Professional Communication: Communicate effectively on complex computing activities with the engineering community and society.
  9. Project Management and Finance: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of engineering management principles.
  10. Lifelong Learning: Recognize the need for and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and lifelong learning.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs) ▼

  1. Core Competency: Develop software solutions using modern programming languages, tools, and technologies.
  2. Domain Expertise: Apply computing techniques in emerging domains like artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, and cybersecurity.
  3. Research and Innovation: Engage in research-driven projects that solve real-world computational challenges.
  4. Interdisciplinary Skills: Integrate computing knowledge with other disciplines to develop holistic technological solutions.
  5. Industry Readiness: Possess skills that align with current industry standards and practices.
